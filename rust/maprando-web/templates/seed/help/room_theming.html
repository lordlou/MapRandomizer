Room theming

This setting controls the graphical appearance of rooms.

  • Vanilla: Rooms appear as they do in the vanilla game.

  • Area Palettes: Rooms colors are adjusted to follow a consistent theme for each area. This provides some amount of variety and cohesiveness in room appearance, while still allowing easy recognizability of rooms.

  • Area Tiling: Rooms graphics are redrawn to follow a consistent theme for each area. This provides the highest level of variety and cohesiveness in room appearance.

Other variations of these options are available under "Other customization options".
