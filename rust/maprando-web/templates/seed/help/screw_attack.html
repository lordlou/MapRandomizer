Screw Attack animation

This setting determines how the Screw Attack animation appears:

  • Vanilla: The Screw Attack animation is always based on the Space Jump animation regardless of whether or not Space Jump is equipped.

  • Split: Screw Attack without Space Jump equipped is based on the spin-jump animation. Screw Attack with Space Jump equipped is based on the Space Jump animation.

The behavior of the vanilla game can be explained by the fact that the player was expected to obtain Space Jump before Screw Attack and never have a reason to unequip Space Jump. Hence the possibility of using Screw Attack without Space Jump was apparently overlooked or considered unimportant.
