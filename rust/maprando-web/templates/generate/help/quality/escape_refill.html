Refill energy for escape

If this option is enabled, then Samus' energy is refilled at the beginning of the escape sequence. This is mainly effective in combination with the "Short" Mother Brain option (which is the default), to compensate for not being refilled by the Baby Metroid.

If this option is disabled, it is possible that the escape may require having collected Reserve Tanks and/or manipulating Mother Brain to end the fight with more energy (up to 340, by damaging down correctly and disabling suits).
