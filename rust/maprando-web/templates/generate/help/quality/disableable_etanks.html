Disableable E-Tanks

If this setting is enabled, the player may disable E-Tanks that they have collected, by navigating to the pause equipment screen, holding Select, and pressing X. By doing this repeatedly, the player may disable as many of their E-Tanks as they would like.

The player may re-enable E-Tanks by again navigating to the pause equipment screen, holding Select and pressing Y. Re-enabled E-Tanks start out empty.
