Initial map reveal

This option controls the extent to which tiles are initially revealed at the start of the game and when visiting a new area.

  • None: No tiles are revealed.
  • Maps: Map stations are revealed after visiting their area.
  • Partial: All tiles are partially revealed, after visiting their area. This shows the outlines of all the rooms in the pause map, while not revealing details such as item dots and door locks.
  • Full: All tiles are fully revealed, after visiting their area.
  • Global: All tiles are fully revealed, across all areas.

Additional options are available for controlling the initial tiles revealed, by clicking the cog on the right. This includes options to reveal save stations, objectives, items, etc.
