Stop item placement early

If enabled, item placement will stop once the game becomes logically beatable. This means that fewer items will be placed at the end of the seed. As a result, some item locations may not contain an item.

In order to ensure the escape is beatable, this option will also force all movement items to be given during the escape, regardless of whether the quality-of-life option "Hyper Beam gives all movement items" is enabled or not.
